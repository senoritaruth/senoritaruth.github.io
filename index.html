Week 1

The first week was grueling. I experienced some obstacles that were completely unexpected, but I have really enjoyed connecting with my peers. We're all very smart and funny, and I enjoy my classmates' contribution. I feel there is a lot of potential for us to help each other a lot, not only technically, but also in terms of motivation and follow-through. The AI talk on Friday was fun, and I liked the fact that it was lead by a CNM board member.

Week 2

Made it through the first crucible. I am so proud of my cohort. No one in this group goes down without a fight. We're all punching up and mostly swinging in the dark, but it feels like all of us are somehow powered by the challenge. I look forward to more with this group, and I'm rooting for every single one of us.

Week 3

Working in teams is always a new and exciting experience. I am flattered by my team to be voted leader, but I am more than ever aware that leadership includes allowing others to find their own leadership voice. I am going to be the guardrails on the side of the road, but I am not going to wrest away from another the desire to develop their own leadership abilities. I will continue to lead by positive reinforcement and the unabashed use of pastries and lunch to reward milestones met, while using suggestion and redirection in times of stress and confusion. One step, one breath at a time.
